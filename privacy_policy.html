Summary: BetterMe is a habit tracking app that stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, share, or transmit your personal information to any external servers.

1. Information We Collect

BetterMe collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Habit names and descriptions you create
  • Progress logs and tracking data
  • Reminder times you set
  • App preferences and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display your habits and track your progress
  • Send local notifications for reminders (if enabled)
  • Generate statistics and insights about your habits
  • Enable data export/import functionality

3. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. Your information:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not uploaded to any cloud servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is not used for analytics or advertising

4. Third-Party Services

BetterMe uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: To display the Outfit font family (font files only, no personal data)
  • Lottie Animations: To display animations (animation files only, no personal data)

These services do not receive any of your personal data or habit information.

5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send habit reminders
  • Storage (optional): To export/import your data

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Export your data using the app's export feature
  • Delete: Delete any habit or all data at any time
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all associated data

7. Children's Privacy

BetterMe does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect any personal information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
